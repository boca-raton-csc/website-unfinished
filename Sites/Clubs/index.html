Sponsor: Ms. Galbo

We plan the fundraising activity for both the children/adults within the Deaf community, at St Augustine school for the Deaf or Palm Beach county Deaf Association. Each school year, the club varies the activities that we will do to raise money to meet the needs within the Deaf community.

Battle of the Books

Meetings: Wednesdays - Room 5106

Sponsor: Ms. Dice

The Book Club reads the Florida Teen List books and discusses them. The books are high interest novels about teen interests and issues. The contest portion involves a team of five taking an online test about the books. The highest scoring teams then compete in person for a Q & A about the books for the Grand Winner. Students make friends and enjoy reading!
